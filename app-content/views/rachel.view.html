Rachel Anderson

After growing up and attending college in Virginia, Rachel kicked off her adult life as a social worker in Atlanta, GA. At age 28, she attending Columbia Theological Seminary and began a journey that would lead her to mission service on the US-Mexico border. During those years she was continually astonished by the amazing grace God provides on the borders of our hearts, lives and nations.

After leaving the border, she was called to a position in the Presbyterian World Mission offices in Louisville, where she continues to serve. Soon after, God began to place a strange tickle in her heart which sparked her desire to serve a congregation. She has been with Mt. Horeb since December 2014 as the part-time pastor.

Rachel's husband, Jim Todd, is a native Kentuckian who splits his time between Mt. Horeb and his family's congregation in Louisville, Advent Episcopal.

Rachel can be reached at rachel.anderson@mthoreblex.org
